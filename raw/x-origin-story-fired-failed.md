# Got Fired from 4 Jobs, Failed 7 Startups, Built $7M Agency

**Date:** 2026-04-03
**Engagement:** 363 likes | 25 replies | 17 retweets | 152 bookmarks | 22,548 views

Got fired from 4 jobs. Failed at 7 startups. Lost $40K of my own savings.

Then I bought coldiq(dot)com in 2020, went full-time in January 2023 with zero client revenue, and said yes to a 3K/month client because I needed the money.

That first client only took 1 to 2 days of work per week. So I kept going.

The ceiling wasn't strategy. It was identity. I kept underpricing myself because I didn't believe I was worth more. Every time revenue grew, I'd hit a wall that had nothing to do with tactics -- it was about who I thought I was.
