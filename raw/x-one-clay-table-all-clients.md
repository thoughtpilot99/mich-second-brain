One Clay table runs across every client at ColdIQ ($7M ARR). It 3X's their TAM.

Five stages, fully automated:

1. Blocklist: ingests your "do-not-contact" list, cleans it, enriches domain URLs, and blocks all enrichments for those domains to save Clay credits

2. Lead sourcing: pulls from multiple data providers via waterfall enrichment

3. Enrichment: adds firmographic, technographic, and intent data

4. Scoring: grades each lead against ICP criteria

5. Routing: pushes qualified leads into the appropriate outreach sequence

Most agencies build 80 Clay tables for 80 clients. We build one.
