# 18 Buying Signals That Tell You Who's Ready to Buy

We've built GTM systems for 300+ B2B companies. The ones booking the most meetings all have one thing in common:

they email people who already want to buy.

Here are 18 buying signals that tell you exactly who's ready to buy:

First-Party Signals (your own ecosystem):
- LinkedIn profile views
- Website visits (anonymous visitor identification)
- Content engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Email opens and clicks
- Webinar/event attendance
- Free trial signups

Third-Party Signals (external data):
- Job postings (hiring for roles you solve)
- Funding events (new budget available)
- Tech stack changes (adopting complementary tools)
- Competitor engagement (following/engaging with competitors)
- Industry event attendance
- Company growth signals (headcount increases)

Intent Signals (research behavior):
- G2/Capterra reviews of competitors
- Keyword searches in your category
- Content downloads from industry publications
- Social media discussions about problems you solve
- Community participation in relevant spaces
- Analyst report downloads
