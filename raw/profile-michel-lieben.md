## Bio

Michel Lieben is CEO and founder of ColdIQ, a B2B go-to-market agency based in Belgium. He bootstrapped ColdIQ from $0 to $7M ARR in 31 months with no outside investors.

Before ColdIQ, Michel was fired from four jobs and failed at seven startups, losing $40K of his own savings. He bought coldiq.com in 2020, went full-time in January 2023 with zero client revenue, and signed his first client at $3K/month.
