Kenny automates entire outbound campaigns using Claude Code.

...and we recorded a 40-min breakdown anyone can replicate:

You write a few prompts in the terminal, along with a list of companies, and then...

Claude Code takes over by:

1. Scoring & segmenting your lead list against your ICP.
2. Finding verified email addresses via several email lookup APIs.
3. Writing personalized copy on its own (based on best performing frameworks)
4. Uploading the whole campaign (leads, sequence & copy) straight into your sequencer.

The entire process is completed in under ~15 minutes.
