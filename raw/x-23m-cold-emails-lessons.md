We've sent over 23M cold emails and built GTM systems for 300+ B2B companies at $7M ARR.

Here's how the top 1% make cold email work in 2026 (based on 1,000,000+ emails analyzed via Instantly):

1. Small Targeted Lists > Big Broad Lists. Micro-lists of 500-1,000 contacts outperform blasts of 10,000+.

2. Stop blasting 10,000 contacts a day. Smaller lists with better data always win.

3. One enriched signal is worth 100 raw emails.

4. AI personalization only works when the data underneath is clean.

5. A 4-step sequence outperforms a 7-step sequence when the targeting is right.
