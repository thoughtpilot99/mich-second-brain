I spent $50K warming prospects with LinkedIn ads before a single cold email went out.

Most people think that's insane. 'Why not just send the emails?'

Because a cold email to someone who's seen your face 5 to 15 times isn't cold anymore.

Reply rates went up without touching a single word of copy.

Prospects who've seen your content 3+ times before receiving a cold email convert at 2-3x the rate. We tested this across several thousand campaigns.

The email didn't sell them. The 6 weeks of LinkedIn posts before it landed is what made them open it.
