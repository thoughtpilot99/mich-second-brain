My cold outreach process that generated 25,000+ leads in 2026 (and got us to $7M ARR with our agency)

1. Build a few relevant audience segments
- Companies engaging with your brand
- Companies similar to your best clients
- Companies showing buying intent in your category

2. Map your total addressable market (TAM)
Every single company that fits your client criteria. Use B2B data sources like Clay, Wiza & Prospeo to build account lists according to criteria (company size, geography, industry).

3. Research your target accounts & segment them into tiers
- Tier 1: dream clients or perfect fits
- Tier 2: good fit clients
- Tier 3: orgs you believe you can help

You can prompt AI models within Clay to tier/score accounts, or do it within Claude Code by creating a .md file that includes your scoring criteria.

4. Try reaching your entire TAM every 3-6 months
- Tier 1 leads: mix manual AE outreach + multichannel engagement (phone via Nooks + LinkedIn + email)
- Tier 2 leads: automate multichannel outreach with AI (Clay + lemlist)
- Tier 3 leads: engage at scale with proper deliverability infrastructure (Instantly)

Pro tip: write the first 25-50 messages manually. Do the unscalable at first; this is how you'll uncover patterns you can scale later on.

Think: "what can I send to my prospect that'll be helpful to him, even if they ignore me" -- What's the first thing I'd do if I signed them as a client? Do it for free.

Example: Building a free 'niche' lead list for target accounts, with companies/people data of their ideal clients.

5. Respond to positive replies ASAP
Faster replies = higher meeting rate. More meetings = more sales.

6. Bonus tips:
- The lead list/targeting is the most important element
- You're interrupting people's day. Make it a nice interruption.
- Implement a proper deliverability setup
- Warm your target accounts with LinkedIn ads before outreach
