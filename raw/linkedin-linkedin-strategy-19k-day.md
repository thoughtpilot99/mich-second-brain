This LinkedIn strategy helps us generate $19,000+/day.

Tips (from growing 2K to 69K followers):

1. Treat your profile like a landing page. Tagline needs social proof. Banner must include your value prop.

2. Get LinkedIn Premium for the custom CTA button. One-click path from post to website.

3. Post at the exact same time every day. Like a TV show. Pick a time, stick to it within 10 minutes.

4. Never schedule your posts. Always hit publish manually. Scheduled posts consistently underperform.

5. Find 2-3 recurring formats you can replicate every week. Proven topic + proven format = consistent performance.

6. Build a swipe file of outlier content in your niche. Use Scripe or Taplio.

7. Mix "yolo" posts with ICP-targeted posts. Min 2 posts/week must address buyer's pain points directly.

8. Carousels can 2x your impressions. Images take up more space in the feed.

9. Repost your content 6 hours after publishing. Delete yesterday's repost, publish today's, repost by evening. Adds 15-20% extra reach.

10. Repurpose your best hits every 2-3 months. Slightly improve each time.

11. Reply to every comment in the first 30 minutes. Engage 15 minutes before posting to warm up.

12. Expand the playbook to your team. 24 people posting added $151K MRR in 90 days for us.
