The biggest lie in cold outreach: more emails = more leads.

We generated 25,000+ leads in 2026 at ColdIQ ($7M ARR). The difference was targeting and tiering, not blasting bigger lists.

1. Segment before you send anything. Companies engaging with your brand, lookalikes of your best clients, companies showing buying intent in your category.

2. Map your total addressable market (TAM). Every single company that fits your client criteria.

3. Research your target accounts & segment them into tiers.
- Tier 1: dream clients or perfect fits (similar to your best clients)
- Tier 2: good fit clients (similar to orgs you helped)
- Tier 3: orgs you believe you can help

4. Try reaching your entire TAM every 3-6 months.
- Tier 1: mix manual AE outreach + multichannel engagement
- Tier 2: automate multichannel outreach with AI (Clay + lemlist)
- Tier 3: engage leads at scale with proper deliverability infrastructure (Instantly)

5. Respond to positive replies ASAP. Faster replies = higher meeting rate.

Bonus tips:
- The lead list/targeting is the most important element
- Implement proper deliverability setup
- Warm target accounts with LinkedIn ads before cold outreach
