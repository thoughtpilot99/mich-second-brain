We've built AI into every layer of GTM across 400+ client campaigns. The teams actually seeing pipeline impact all did the same five things:

1. Engineered context before prompts. AI has live access to CRM, enrichment, transcripts, and campaign data before it acts.

2. Connected tools into workflows, not isolated experiments.

3. Used AI for research and personalization, not just email writing.

4. Built scoring models that learn from campaign results.

5. Kept humans in the loop for high-value decisions.
