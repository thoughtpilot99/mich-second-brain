# The Exact Playbook: $0 to $7M ARR in 31 Months

**Source:** X | https://twitter.com/MichLieben/status/2031030803388629490
**Date:** 2026-03 (approx)
**Engagement:** Major thread

---

ColdIQ went from zero revenue to $7M ARR without raising a single dollar. No VC, no angel round, no safety net. Just cold email, LinkedIn content, and a system built one phase at a time.

What works at one revenue stage actively holds you back at a higher stage. Every phase required a completely different playbook.

The entire business was built on the idea that content and outbound aren't separate strategies. They're two halves of the same engine.

Started ColdIQ as an affiliate content site writing about sales tools. When people kept asking him to run their outbound instead of just reviewing the tools, he eventually said yes. First client came from a two-week cold email campaign that closed at $4.8K/month.

After writing about it on LinkedIn, the post picked up traction and three more clients showed up within days.

At $15.7K/month, a co-founder (Alex Vacca) joined from Worldcoin. They split the business into two lanes: one focused entirely on generating demand and bringing in revenue, the other on building delivery systems that could scale. This was "the single decision that made everything else possible."

By March 2024, six team members posting consistently on LinkedIn. The combination of regular publishing and outbound campaigns created a compounding effect.

For agencies starting today, the fastest path to $15K/month is combining outbound with content about the work being done. Blog posts and cold emails generated the first three clients. LinkedIn generated the next three.

Now at $7M ARR with 30 full-time employees and over 267 B2B clients served.
