How to turn $233,000 in LinkedIn ad spend into $1,500,000+ in closed revenue?

AirOps had a great product trusted by Webflow, Ramp, Carta and LegalZoom. But their pipeline had been stuck at ~$536K/month for almost a year.

We built a full ABM motion combining outbound + LinkedIn Ads, hitting the same accounts from every angle.

Account-Based Outbound:
- Building and tiering target account lists with Clay using fit + intent signals
- Syncing outbound and LinkedIn Ads to hit the same companies from both sides
- Signal-triggered plays that changed based on account engagement stage

Thought Leader Ads:
- Put 80% of ad spend behind founder content, not product ads
- Used webinars as an outbound play -- 112 positive replies from input-request invites
- Cross-channel feedback loops between outbound and paid

Results:
- $7.8M qualified pipeline
- $1.5M closed-won revenue
- 164 deals created, 30 closed-won
- 15x pipeline ROI on $233K ad spend
- 1,058 outbound replies
- Ad spend scaled 20x with positive ROI
