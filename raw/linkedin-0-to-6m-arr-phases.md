# ColdIQ: $0 to $6M+ ARR in 31 Months - Six Phases

**Source:** LinkedIn | https://www.linkedin.com/posts/michel-lieben_coldiq-went-from-0-to-6000000-arr-in-activity-7383813893648973824-2fBy
**Date:** 2026-01 (approx)
**Engagement:** High engagement

---

ColdIQ went from $0 to $6,000,000+ ARR in 31 months. Six phases:

Phase 1 ($0 to $15,700/mo): Blog + cold emails got first 3 clients. LinkedIn content got the next 3. Started as affiliate content site reviewing sales tools; people kept asking to run their outbound.

Phase 2 ($15,700 to $30K/mo): Alex joined May 2023. Increased content output from both founders generated significant inbound, had to pause outreach.

Phase 3 ($30K to $70K/mo): Added GTM Engineers. Six team members posting consistently by March 2024. Reached ~100 qualified monthly meetings.

Phase 4 ($70K to $250K/mo): Soheil arrived as Head of Delivery. Launched coaching, LinkedIn Ads, email infrastructure services.

Phase 5 ($250K to $500K/mo): Integrated content, cold outreach, and ads through "Allbound." Monthly posts went from 40 to 100+.

Phase 6: Acknowledges challenges when team members left to start competing agencies.

Tools used: Taplio, Claude, Grammarly, Notion.
