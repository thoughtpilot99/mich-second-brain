# From $40K/month to $90K/month: The Allbound Shift

**Source:** X | https://twitter.com/MichLieben/status/2040174538999439579
**Date:** 2026-04-03
**Engagement:** 49 likes | 2 replies | 6 retweets | 102 bookmarks | 11,468 views

---

We hit $40K/month running outbound as a single channel. Stayed there for months.

More emails didn't move the number. Better copy didn't either.

The jump to $90K/month came from one shift:

we stopped treating channels like separate strategies.

> LinkedIn warms prospects before cold email lands
> Ads amplify proven content to exact ICPs being prospected
> Outbound targets accounts already engaging with content

Content and outbound aren't separate strategies. They're two halves of the same engine.
