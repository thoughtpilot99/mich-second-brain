# Allbound

[[ColdIQ]]'s integrated GTM framework where content, outbound, and paid ads operate as one unified engine.

> "Content and outbound aren't separate strategies -- they're two halves of the same engine."
> -- [[Michel Lieben]]

## How It Works

1. **Content layer**: [[Michel Lieben]] and team publish on LinkedIn (see [[LinkedIn Content Engine]], [[Employee Advocacy]]). Builds awareness among ICP.
2. **Ad layer**: LinkedIn ad spend pushes top content to exact accounts being prospected. Prospects who see content 3+ times before cold email convert at 2-3x rate (see [[Content to Pipeline]]).
3. **Outbound layer**: [[Signal-Based Outbound]] identifies buying signals. [[Cold Email Deliverability]] ensures inbox landing. [[Value-First Outreach]] leads with a gift.

## Revenue Impact

[[ColdIQ]] jumped from $40K/month to $90K/month after shifting to Allbound. The breakthrough was not doing more of any single channel but making all channels aware of each other and orchestrating them around the same accounts. This corresponds to Phase 4-5 in [[Agency Growth Phases]].

## Why It Works

- Trust compounds across touchpoints
- Signal density increases when all channels generate engagement data
- Cost per acquisition drops when warming via content is cheaper than brute-forcing cold outbound
- The [[GTM Engineer]] role operationalizes Allbound using [[Clay]], [[Instantly]], [[Lemlist]], and [[Claude Code]]
