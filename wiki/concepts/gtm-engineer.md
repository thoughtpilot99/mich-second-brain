# GTM Engineer

A new role that runs [[ColdIQ]]'s entire outbound operation. Did not exist three years ago. Hybrid of sales ops and engineering.

## What They Do

GTM Engineers use [[Clay]], [[Claude Code]], and [[n8n]] to build automated go-to-market systems. They replace the manual work traditionally done by SDR teams while keeping humans in the loop for high-value decisions.

Kenny Damian demonstrated automating a full outbound campaign in under 15 minutes: scoring leads, finding emails, writing personalized copy, and uploading to the sequencer.

## Economics

"AI transformed how SDRs work. Same pipeline, 71% less cost." SDRs focus on relationships; engineers handle systems. This is why [[ColdIQ]] can operate at scale with 35 FT employees serving 267+ clients.

## Tools of the Trade

- [[Clay]] for data enrichment and [[Waterfall Enrichment]]
- [[Claude Code]] with 58+ GTM skills for automation
- [[n8n]] for workflow orchestration
- [[Instantly]], [[Lemlist]], [[Smartlead]] for email delivery

The GTM Engineer is the role that operationalizes [[Allbound]] and [[Signal-Based Outbound]].
