# Tiered Prospecting

Segmenting your Total Addressable Market (TAM) into tiers before outreach, then matching the intensity and method of outreach to each tier.

## The Three Tiers

- **Tier 1** (dream clients): Manual AE outreach + multichannel. Phone (Nooks) + LinkedIn + email. Write the first 25-50 messages by hand to find patterns.
- **Tier 2** (good fits): Automated multichannel via [[Clay]] + [[Lemlist]]. Every message must be valuable, relevant, and personalized. See [[Value-First Outreach]].
- **Tier 3** (broader market): Scale via [[Instantly]] with proper deliverability infrastructure. Use as experimental playground to test copywriting frameworks.

## Key Insight

[[ColdIQ]] cut 40% of their TAM on purpose before sending a single email. Reply rates went up. The counterintuitive lesson: less volume, better targeting = more pipeline. See [[Lead List as Strategy]].

Reach your entire TAM every 3-6 months rather than blasting once. Combined with [[Signal-Based Outbound]], this creates a systematic coverage model.
