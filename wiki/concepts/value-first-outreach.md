# Value-First Outreach

Lead with value in cold outreach rather than a pitch.

> "What's the first thing I'd do if I signed them as a client? Do it for free."
> -- [[Michel Lieben]]

## How It Works

Instead of pitching your service, provide something tangible the prospect can use immediately. Example: pre-build a targeted lead list for a recruitment firm, showing the methodology and sample data.

Results: "outbound leads that book meetings inbound" -- prospects self-schedule because the free value demonstrates competence better than any pitch.

This is the Tier 2 outreach philosophy in [[Tiered Prospecting]], executed at scale via [[Clay]] and [[Lemlist]]. When combined with [[Content to Pipeline]] (where prospects already recognize you), value-first emails convert dramatically higher than traditional cold outreach.
