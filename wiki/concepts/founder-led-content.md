# Founder-Led Content

Founders posting on LinkedIn as a primary growth channel. [[Michel Lieben]] went from 0 to 55K+ followers. Breakthrough came on post #31: 600+ likes, 140K impressions, 25 meetings, 4 clients.

## Evolution

2024 was the year of founder-led content. 2025+ is about [[Employee Advocacy]] -- employees posting scales reach beyond what any single founder can achieve. Most LinkedIn users aren't founders; they relate more to practitioners.

## ColdIQ Results

[[ColdIQ]]: 12M+ impressions, equivalent to $360K in ad value, 1,000+ qualified inbound meetings, ~$150K/month in MRR. Six team members posting consistently by March 2024.

The key insight: founder-led content builds the initial audience and credibility, then [[Employee Advocacy]] scales it. Both feed into [[Content to Pipeline]] and the broader [[Allbound]] motion.

## Consistency Over Quality

"You will not know what a good post looks like until you have published at least 100 posts." Michel posted 30+ times before landing his first lead. Needed three attempts to establish consistent publishing. See [[LinkedIn Content Engine]] for the tactical playbook.
