# LinkedIn Content Engine

[[ColdIQ]]'s systematic approach to LinkedIn publishing that generates $19,000+/day in revenue.

## Core Rules

1. Post at the exact same time every day (like a TV show)
2. Never schedule posts -- always publish manually (scheduled posts underperform)
3. Find 2-3 recurring formats to replicate weekly
4. Build a swipe file of outlier content using [[Taplio]] or Scripe
5. Mix personal ("yolo") posts with ICP-targeted posts (min 2/week addressing buyer pain)
6. Invest in visuals -- 1050x1380 images take up the full screen, 2-3x engagement
7. Repurpose best hits every 2-3 months, slightly improved each time
8. Reply to every comment within 30 minutes
9. Repost content 6 hours after publishing for 15-20% extra reach
10. Engage 15 minutes before posting to warm up your account
11. Expand to team (see [[Employee Advocacy]])

## Visual Strategy

[[Michel Lieben]] has generated over $2M from LinkedIn infographics. Text-only posts get two lines in the feed; a full-size image takes the entire screen.
