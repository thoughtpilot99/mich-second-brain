# Agency Growth Phases

[[ColdIQ]]'s journey from $0 to $7M ARR in 31 months, broken into six phases. What works at one stage actively holds you back at the next.

## Phase 1: $0 to $15.7K/month

Blog + cold emails got first 3 clients. LinkedIn content got the next 3. Started as an affiliate site reviewing sales tools; people kept asking [[Michel Lieben]] to run their outbound. First client: $3K/month (see [[The Identity Ceiling]]).

## Phase 2: $15.7K to $30K/month

[[Alex Vacca]] joined May 2023 from Worldcoin. Split into two lanes: demand gen (Alex) and delivery systems (Michel). Content output ramped; had to pause outreach due to inbound volume.

## Phase 3: $30K to $70K/month

Added [[GTM Engineer]] roles. Six team members posting on LinkedIn by March 2024. ~100 qualified monthly meetings.

## Phase 4: $70K to $250K/month

Head of Delivery (Soheil) arrived. Launched coaching, LinkedIn Ads, email infrastructure services. Multiple service lines.

## Phase 5: $250K to $500K/month

Integrated all channels through [[Allbound]]. Monthly posts went from 40 to 100+. [[Employee Advocacy]] became a growth lever.

## Phase 6: $500K+/month

Scaling challenges. Some team members left to start competing agencies. Revenue hit $7M ARR.

## Key Lesson

"What works at $15K/month will actively hold you back at $250K/month." Each phase required a completely different playbook. Tools: [[Taplio]], [[Claude Code]], Grammarly, Notion.
