# Content to Pipeline

The mechanism by which LinkedIn content engagement converts into revenue, especially when combined with cold outbound.

## The Core Insight

Prospects who've seen your content 3+ times before receiving a cold email convert at 2-3x the normal rate. [[Michel Lieben]] tested this across several thousand campaigns.

> "The email didn't sell them. The 6 weeks of LinkedIn posts before it landed is what made them open it."

## The $50K LinkedIn Ads Play

[[ColdIQ]] spent $50K promoting best LinkedIn posts through ads -- not to generate inbound leads, but to make cold email convert better. When prospects have seen your face 5-15 times before the email lands, reply rates increase without changing a word of copy.

## AirOps Case Study

[[AirOps]]: $233K LinkedIn ad spend -> $1.5M closed-won revenue, $7.8M pipeline. 80% of ad spend went behind founder content (thought leader ads), not product ads. 15x pipeline ROI.

This is the execution layer of [[Allbound]] and a key reason [[Employee Advocacy]] drives real revenue, not just vanity metrics.
