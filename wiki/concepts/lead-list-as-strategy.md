# Lead List as Strategy

"The list is the strategy." Targeting and segmentation matter more than copywriting. Most people spend too little time on list building.

## The Principle

From 23M+ cold emails: micro-lists of 500-1,000 contacts outperform blasts of 10,000+. [[ColdIQ]] cut 40% of their TAM on purpose before sending a single email -- reply rates went up.

## How to Build

1. Research first 25-50 prospects manually to find patterns (job changes, tech stack, hiring signals)
2. Use those patterns to build automated scoring in [[Clay]] or [[Claude Code]]
3. Segment into [[Tiered Prospecting]] tiers before writing a single email
4. Enrich via [[Waterfall Enrichment]] for maximum data coverage

The biggest killer of cold email is not bad copy -- it's bad targeting. Putting your message in front of the right person matters more than what the message says. See [[Signal-Based Outbound]] for how to find the right people at the right time.
