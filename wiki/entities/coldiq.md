# ColdIQ

B2B go-to-market agency founded by [[Michel Lieben]] (CEO) and [[Alex Vacca]] (COO). Bootstrapped to $7M ARR in 31 months.

## Overview

ColdIQ helps B2B companies build and scale outbound, content, and paid acquisition. 267+ clients served, 35 full-time + 12 fractional employees.

## Services

1. **Outbound** -- cold email and LinkedIn outreach using [[Clay]], [[Instantly]], [[Lemlist]], [[Smartlead]]
2. **LinkedIn Content** -- ghostwriting and strategy using [[Taplio]]
3. **LinkedIn Ads** -- paid campaigns targeting B2B buyers
4. **Coaching** -- training teams to run GTM playbooks independently

## The [[Allbound]] Approach

ColdIQ's core methodology blends content, outbound, and ads into a unified GTM motion. See [[Allbound]] for details.
