# Alex Vacca

COO and co-founder of [[ColdIQ]]. Joined in May 2023 from Worldcoin (Sam Altman's biometric identity project). X: @itsalexvacca.

## Role at ColdIQ

Alex handles demand generation and day-to-day operations. While [[Michel Lieben]] focuses on strategy and brand, Alex runs the machine that turns inbound interest into revenue. His joining at $15.7K/month was described as "the single decision that made everything else possible" (see [[Agency Growth Phases]]).

## LinkedIn Growth

Grew from 4,000 to 24,000+ LinkedIn followers in one year using [[ColdIQ]]'s own content playbook and [[Taplio]].
