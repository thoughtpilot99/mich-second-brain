# Michel Lieben

CEO and founder of [[ColdIQ]], a B2B go-to-market agency. Based in Belgium.

## Background

Michel was fired from four jobs and failed at seven startups before building [[ColdIQ]] into a $7M ARR business, bootstrapped, in 31 months. He bought coldiq.com in 2020, went full-time in January 2023 with zero client revenue, and signed his first client at $3K/month because he needed the money. See [[The Identity Ceiling]] for his perspective on why.

## Social Presence

- **LinkedIn:** 55,000-65,000+ followers. Top 1% creator in Belgium. Treats LinkedIn as a primary demand-gen channel.
- **X (Twitter):** @MichLieben
- **YouTube:** @MichLieben

## Role at ColdIQ

Michel oversees strategy, builds the public knowledge base around outbound and GTM engineering, and architects automation workflows. He works alongside [[Alex Vacca]] (COO/co-founder) who handles demand gen and operations.

## Key Contributions

- Pioneered [[ColdIQ]]'s [[Allbound]] methodology combining outbound, content, and ads
- Built and open-sourced GTM automation workflows using [[Claude Code]], [[n8n]], [[Clay]], and [[Relevance AI]]
- Gave away a Claude Project that builds [[n8n]] workflows from plain-language prompts
- Developed 58+ GTM skills for [[Claude Code]]
- Ran the [[Employee Advocacy]] competition that generated $153K MRR in 90 days

## Philosophy

Michel's content emphasizes: bootstrapping over fundraising, systems over hustle, AI-augmented GTM over manual prospecting, and radical transparency about failures. His origin story is a recurring narrative. Core belief: "Content and outbound aren't separate strategies -- they're two halves of the same engine."
