# AirOps

AI workflow platform. Major [[ColdIQ]] client case study. Pipeline was stuck at ~$536K/month. [[ColdIQ]] built a full ABM motion combining outbound + LinkedIn Ads. Results: $7.8M qualified pipeline, $1.5M closed-won revenue, 164 deals created, 15x pipeline ROI on $233K ad spend. Demonstrates [[Allbound]] and [[Content to Pipeline]] in action.
