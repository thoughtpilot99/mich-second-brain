# Taplio

LinkedIn content tool for inspiration, analytics, and scheduling. Key tool in [[ColdIQ]]'s [[LinkedIn Content Engine]]. Used to study top performers, build swipe files, and plan content calendars. [[Alex Vacca]]'s growth from 4K to 24K+ followers was supported by Taplio. Also used alongside Scripe for content ideation.
