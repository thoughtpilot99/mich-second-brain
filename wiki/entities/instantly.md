# Instantly

Email outreach platform. Key partner of [[ColdIQ]]. Core differentiator: unlimited email accounts at fixed pricing ($37-$97/month). Built-in email warmup. G2 rating: 4.9/5.

In [[ColdIQ]]'s stack, Instantly handles high-volume Tier 3 sending (see [[Tiered Prospecting]]), while [[Lemlist]] covers multichannel sequences and [[Smartlead]] optimizes for reply rates.
